My Research

My research interests lie at the intersection of syntax, semantics, and information structure, with a particular focus on Mandarin Chinese. I am interested in how linguistic forms encode meaning and how information is packaged and conveyed in discourse.

Spurious ta: the Non-Pronomunal Use of a Pronoun in Mandarin Chinese

This project investigates the phonological form ta in Mandarin Chinese and its use as a scalar focus-sensitive particle.
