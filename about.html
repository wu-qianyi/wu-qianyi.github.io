About Me

I'm a linguist-in-training - an MPhil student in Linguistics at University College London. I'm interested in syntax, syntax-semantics interface, and computational linguistics.

Currently, I'm working on Mandarin syntax. To be specific, fragments: I am trying to find a way to account for the fragment answers, questions and corrections. The research section has a more detailed description.

Outside linguistics, I enjoy reading and watching things, especially sitcoms and comedies.

I occasionally write about linguistics and other things on my blog (still under construction).
